# Altair

Altair is an **AI-driven intelligence analysis platform** designed to reveal hidden connections and patterns in publicly available data. Inspired by advanced intelligence operations, it ingests open-source datasets, extracts entities and relationships with cutting-edge NLP, and maps them in a dynamic knowledge graph. The platform generates actionable intelligence, interactive visualizations, and research-ready insights â€” transforming raw data into clarity and foresight.

---

## Features

- **OSINT Data Collection:** Aggregates structured and unstructured datasets from multiple public sources.
- **Entity Extraction & NLP:** Uses AI models (spaCy, HuggingFace Transformers) to identify entities, events, and hidden relationships.
- **Knowledge Graph Visualization:** Maps complex networks of entities in **Neo4j** for pattern discovery and link analysis.
- **AI-Driven Insights:** Generates summaries, predicts connections, highlights anomalies, and produces actionable intelligence reports.
- **Interactive Dashboard:** Explore relationships through graphs, maps, and timelines, just like a high-tech intelligence control room.
- **Research-Focused Design:** Fully reproducible and extensible for academic research and cybersecurity applications.
